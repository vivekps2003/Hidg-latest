## 🔍 WHY YOU DON'T SEE BUTTONS

### The Code is 100% Correct!

The buttons ARE implemented. They just don't show because:

**Your orders have wrong status!**

Check your Firebase:
1. Open Firebase Console
2. Go to Firestore → orders collection
3. Look at the `status` field

**If status is:**
- `pending` → Only Accept/Reject buttons show
- `accepted` → Only Manage Pickup button shows
- `completed` → Only completed banner shows

**You need:**
- `picked_up` → To see "Verify Weight Now" button
- `verified` → To see "Process Payment" button
